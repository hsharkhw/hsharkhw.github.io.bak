About Me

I am a Ph.D. candidate in the Department of Computer Science at Tsinghua University, advised by Prof. Lifeng Sun and Prof. Zhi Wang. Before that, I received my B.E. degree in School of Computer Science and Technology from Xidian Universiy in 2012. My research work focuses on the multimedia network optimization, including server selection, overlay path construction, edge-assisted content delivery. For more details, please refer to my CV.
